My Life In Numbers

 

My Life In Numbers displays real-time calculations using  interesting statistics, fun facts and fascinating numbers.


We measure all sorts of events in many different ways. For example, we are currently counting down to the 2014 FIFA World Cup in Lionel Messi's earnings and measuring the earth solar distance since the first moon landing. Please check out the Live Events page.


You can create your own personal events so you could measure how many seconds old you are (do you know when you will be (or were) a billion seconds old?), how many heartbeats you’ve had since you met your partner, how much money the richest man in the world has earned since you started your job or how many flights there are until you go on holiday.


We also have a free iPhone app available for when you are on the move and don’t forget to check out the blog to see what’s coming up next. Thanks.
